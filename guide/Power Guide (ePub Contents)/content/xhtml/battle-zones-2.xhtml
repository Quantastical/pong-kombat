Forest

Enormous trees and vegetation grow from the ground up beyond the playing area. The plant life rustles in the wind and as animals and rodents scurry across the landscape below.

Dark shadows and the trees above dance in the wind, limiting visibility.

Hell

Hell is a pit of fire and skulls. The blazing inferno causes explosions of liquid hot magma and bubbling rivers of lava are below.

The insane heat causes players to have to keep their paddles in motion. Sitting in one area too long will slowly deplete their health.
