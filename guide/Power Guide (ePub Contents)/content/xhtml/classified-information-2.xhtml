Hidden Paddles

From Agent #8253

Hold the the Up button or any direction for more than 3 seconds when choosing your paddle to toggle a second page of playable characters, including bosses and secret paddles. All of the paddles other than main characters must be unlocked by defeating them at least once.

Pacintosh System 1

From Agent #8486

On some websites, when viewing the contents of a directory, the end-user is presented with a standard index listing that provides hyperlinks to all of the files within it. On PongKombat.com, the directory listing resembles an old black-and-white graphical user interface, complete with sizable windows, a working clock, and even a functional calculator.

Spamality Dismantle

From Agent #51486

Spamalities are special finishing moves that can be performed by any character and result in a comedic representation of life with spam, and it's not just about the processed meat-type food.

Super Pong Kombat

From Agent #32582

Play a single-player Pong Kombat skinned version of the classic Arkanoid game is unlocked after completing the single player tournament with each of the five main characters. In it, players volley a ball inside of a playing field filled blocks. Clear the blocks from the playing field by bouncing the ball off them or sending a projectile their way.

God Mode

From Agent #51383

Enter the, now infamous, Konami Code button sequence on the title screen, before opening the title menu to bypass all of the tournament competitors prior to White Paddle.
