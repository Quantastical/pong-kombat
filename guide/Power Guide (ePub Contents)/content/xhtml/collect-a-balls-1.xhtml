Collect-a-Balls

Gotta Unlock 'em All!

At the start of the match, and after failure to return a volley, a ball is put into the center of the playing field and launches at a random angle. When balls are put into play, they are randomly selected from a pool of available options. Each ball has a distinct size and bouncy-ness to them. New balls are unlocked over time, based on the number of matches you have completed.
