Power-Ups

In the middle of the playing field, Power-Up special abilities will pop up from time to time during battle. Race your competitor to pick them up to gain an upper hand against them.

Meat

Pick up the Meat power-up and you will regain some life.

Glue

After collecting the Glue power-up, the ball will get stuck to your paddle for a short period of time, enabling you to strategically toss it back by pressing the Action button.

Energy Drink

Pick up the Energy Drink power-up to enjoy a temporary speed increase, allowing you to avoid attacks and return volleys a bit more easily.

Shield

The Shield power-up protects your paddle from your competitors projectiles, but still allowing you to return the ball volley.

Stopwatch

As the ball approaches your side of the playing area, the Stopwatch power-up causes it's speed to decrease, giving you more time to react.
