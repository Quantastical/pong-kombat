Storm

The paddles duel high above the city streets admist a heavy storm.

Strong winds of the storm cause the ball's speed to increase or decrease as it travels across the screen. Frequent lightning strikes cause flashes of light that block large portions of the playing field.

"Shock To The System" Right, Right, Left, Action

Tower

The six-story Tower arena is a wooden floored structure built atop a cement basement.

Ropes hanging above dangle to hinder visibility, while the bell's shadow serves as a distraction while tolls.

"Going Down" Down, Right, Up, Action
