As you progress, new balls are awarded less frequently, as to follow a pattern known as the Fibonacci sequence. Balls will be unlocked after 1 match, again after 2 matches, then 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, 34, 55, and so forth.

Fn = Fn - 1 + Fn - 2

Balls can also be unlocked by encountering them while playing against a human opponent in a Paddle-2-Paddle match.
