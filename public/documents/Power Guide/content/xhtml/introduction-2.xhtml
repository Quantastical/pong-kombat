The popular freeware parody returns!

Originally developed as a school project by Stefan Gagne in 1994, Pong Kombat is reimagined in HTML5, twenty years later. Developed by Jeff Jenkins (Quantastical.com), the Pong Kombat rebirth adheres to the original principals and concepts, incorporates popular features of the sequels (Pong Kombat 2 and Pong Kombat 3), while adding in new elements and enhancements. Learn new abilities and dismantles, find hidden secrets, and for the first time in the series, compete on-line in the all new Paddle-2-Paddle mode.
